Co-Academic Transformative Framework for IT Student - By Prof. Rahul M. Samant | HOD-IT, NBNSTIC

Year-wise interactive dashboard: Mindset, Skills, Stimuli, Department activities.

Year 1 — Foundation & Orientation
Objective: Shift students to self-driven, professional-oriented learning.
Mindset Formation
Overview

Mindset & Attitude Goals

  • Curiosity, openness to learning
  • Discipline, punctuality, responsibility
  • Collaborative teamwork and peer respect

Skills Focus

  • Introductory programming (Python, C)
  • Computing fundamentals, digital logic, mathematics
  • Basic communication and presentation
  • Introduction to Git, Linux, IDEs

Teacher Stimuli & Activities

  • Motivational talks and industry orientation
  • Weekly mini coding challenges
  • Group projects (exploratory, non‑graded)
  • Peer‑led learning circles and senior mentoring
Department activities
Coding Bootcamp — Semester 1 intensive
Communication Workshop — Early semester
Peer Mentoring — Ongoing
Year 2 — Skill Development & Exploration
Objective: Strengthen core skills; initiate project-based learning.
Project-based
Overview

Mindset & Attitude Goals

  • Self-directed study beyond syllabus
  • Embrace iterative failure and experimentation
  • Consistency and reflective practice

Skills Focus

  • Data structures & algorithms, DBMS
  • Web & mobile application basics
  • Technical report writing and research basics
  • Version control and collaborative workflows

Teacher Stimuli

  • Flipped classroom with student presentations
  • Course-end mini-projects and code reviews
  • Encourage competitive coding & hackathon prep
Department activities
Internal Hackathon — Biannual
Industrial Visits — Targeted to core domains
Project Exhibition Day — Semester end
Year 3 — Professionalism & Specialization
Objective: Industry-ready competencies & specialization.
Specialization
Overview

Mindset & Attitude Goals

  • Ownership of career direction
  • Resilience and adaptability
  • Professional networking and collaboration

Skills Focus

  • Advanced programming, system design
  • Electives: Cloud, AI/ML, Cybersecurity, DevOps
  • Open-source contribution and technical writing

Teacher Stimuli

  • Independent research-based projects
  • Industry-level case studies and critiques
  • Mock interviews, resume clinics and mentorship
Department activities
Mandatory Summer Internship — Secured & monitored
Industry Mentor Program — Pairing scheme
Student Research Conference — Project showcases
Year 4 — Leadership & Career Readiness
Objective: Leadership, entrepreneurship, final consolidation.
Capstone
Overview

Mindset & Attitude Goals

  • Leadership, ownership and innovation
  • Resilience in uncertainty (startup/job)
  • Ethical responsibility in technology

Skills Focus

  • Capstone project (industry/research partnered)
  • Advanced technical specializations
  • Entrepreneurial skills — pitching, business plans

Teacher Stimuli

  • Industry capstone guidance and continuous feedback
  • Alumni sessions on careers and failures
  • Support for entrepreneurship and funding pathways
Department activities
Final Project Exhibition + Pitching Event
Placement Readiness Program — Mock tests, HR rounds
Innovation & Entrepreneurship Cell — Showcases
